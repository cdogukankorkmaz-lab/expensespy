Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Your privacy matters. ExpenseSpy collects only what's necessary to deliver the service. We never sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name and email (via Google/Apple Sign-In or email/password).
  • Receipt data: Photos you upload and extracted data (date, amount, merchant, category, items).
  • Usage data: Basic analytics to improve the product (features used, crash reports).
  • Payment information: Subscription status via Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain the ExpenseSpy service
  • Process receipt photos using AI/OCR technology
  • Sync your data securely across your devices
  • Manage your subscription and account
  • Send important service notifications (not marketing)

3. Receipt Photos & AI Processing

Receipt photos are sent to OpenAI Vision API solely for data extraction. Images are not used to train AI models or shared beyond this processing. All extracted data is stored in your personal Firebase account, accessible only by you.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • All data encrypted at rest (Firebase / Google Cloud)
  • Authentication handled via Firebase Auth
  • We do not store passwords

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data for marketing. We share data only with: Firebase/Google Cloud (storage), OpenAI (receipt processing only), RevenueCat (subscription management), and when required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data in the app at any time
  • Export: Export receipts as Excel, CSV or PDF anytime
  • Delete: Delete individual receipts or your entire account at any time. All data permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

ExpenseSpy is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date above and via in-app notification.

9. Contact

Have questions about this policy? Send us a message through the app — Settings → Contact Us.
